Objective

  • Review basic principles and processes for optimizing the accessibility of document content in Microsoft Word and PowerPoint.

Sections

This module has five Sections:

  • Section 1: Overview of Document Accessibility
  • Section 2: Images
  • Section 3: Hyperlinks
  • Section 4: Contrast & Color Reliance
  • Section 5: Optimizing Writing

Each Section has a Quiz with multiple-choice questions. You should answer all Section Quiz questions correctly before moving on to the next Section.

After you have completed all of the Section Quizzes, take the Exam.

Exam

Exam 1 has ten questions that have been randomly selected from each of the Module 1 Section Quizzes.
