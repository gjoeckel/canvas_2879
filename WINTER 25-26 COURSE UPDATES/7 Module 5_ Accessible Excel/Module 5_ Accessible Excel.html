Objective

Review basic principles and processes for optimizing document structure, and for utilizing a built-in accessibility checker, in Excel documents.

Sections

This module has six Sections:

  • Section 1: Navigating in Excel
  • Section 2: Sheets & Tables
  • Section 3: Images & Links
  • Section 4: Charts
  • Section 5: Optimizing Workbooks
  • Section 6: Evaluating Accessibility in Excel

Each Section has a Quiz with multiple-choice questions. You should answer all Section Quiz questions correctly before moving on to the next Section.

After you have completed all of the Section Quizzes, take the Exam.

Exam

Exam 5 has ten questions that have been randomly selected from each of the Module 5 Section Quizzes.
